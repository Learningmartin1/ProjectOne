These are the video game companies and music artist that inspires me:

Sony Interactive Entertainment

The games that Sony Interactive Entertainment had made all kinds of games that I played on my Playstation systems such as Jak and Daxter, Sly Cooper, and Ratchet & Clank.
The gameplay that the company designed for each of those games is what inspired me to write good stories on fanfiction websites.

Insomniac Games

Insomniac Games have made some good games such as the Marvel's Spider-Man franchise and the new Ratchet & Clank game. I like the artwork that the artists made for the Spider-Man games.
I also like graphic and art design on all of the Spider-Man games and other games developed by this company and wonder if I can make good art like the artists that work for Insomniac Games.

Keke Palmer

I admire the songs that Keke Palmer had sung in her albums over the years. The songs that I listened to were both inspirational and unique.
Each of her songs are both expressive and has a clear message to everyone that listens to them. I guess that some of Keke's music has a lot of meaning in them as well.
